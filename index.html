About

I’m Connor Rankin — a videographer and VFX artist building cinematic work across commercial-style content, music-driven edits, and visual effects shots. I focus on clean composition, intentional camera movement, and strong pacing, with an eye toward integrating VFX seamlessly into live action.

My background spans on-set videography (gimbal, handheld, drone), post-production editing and finishing, and VFX workflows including compositing, environment creation, and camera tracking. This site is a curated reference of my work for collaborations and job opportunities.
