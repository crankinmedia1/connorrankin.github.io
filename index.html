About Connor

I’m Connor Rankin — a Tucson-based videographer and VFX artist with a Film & Television background from the University of Arizona. I create cinematic commercial-style content with a focus on intentional camera movement, clean composition, and strong pacing.

Professionally, I work across drone, real estate, event, and corporate video — while continuing to build deeper into VFX workflows including compositing, environment creation, and camera tracking.
