About

I’m Connor Rankin. A Tucson-based videographer, photographer, and VFX artist with a Film & Television background from the University of Arizona. I create cinematic commercial-style content with a focus on intentional camera movement, clean composition, and strong pacing.

Professionally, I work across drone, real estate, event, corporate video, and photography, while continuing to build deeper into VFX workflows including compositing, environment creation, and camera tracking. My goal is simple: make work that feels polished, cinematic, and seamlessly integrated.
